**Project description (under 200 words):**

Argus is a trading research companion built on 0G. Before a trader enters a position, they run their thesis through an AI research session powered by 0G Compute. The AI challenges their reasoning, asks clarifying questions and surfaces relevant past analyses from their vault.

Every session is stored permanently on 0G Storage, signed by the user's wallet and timestamped on-chain. The vault is a searchable, immutable record of every analysis the trader has ever made.

The signal journal lets traders log structured pre-trade entries. The AI reviews each entry, returns a critique and assigns a review status of approved, flagged or rejected. Journal entries are stored alongside research sessions in the same on-chain vault.

Pattern recall means that when a new session opens, the AI retrieves relevant past analyses and references them in the response. The longer a trader uses Argus, the more useful the AI becomes because it knows their full history.

0G does real work in all three pillars: Compute for AI inference, Storage for the permanent vault, Chain for verifiable timestamps. Remove 0G and Argus does not function.
